    Beginners Vdeo Editing Bootcamp

    Go from novice to PRO

    Bootcamp Start Date: September 6th

    Course Description

    The two weeks beginners bootcamp will be centered on the industry standard video editing software, Adobe Premiere Pro CC. This course will take you through the fundamentals of video editing, how to take your shots, editing video and audio, correcting color, adding titles and effects, working with green screen, and lots more. The course begins with how to record good footages and then goes through the entire post-production workflow, including media import, editing and trimming, audio adjustment, applying effects, and exporting. By the end of the course, you should be able to create your own video projects with Premiere Pro.

    The course is designed in such a way that you get to be directly invoved in the entire process, as you get to record your own footages and do the editing yourself, while being guided by the facilitator. At the end of this course, you should be able to produce professional video content for your desired media goal.

    Price: N5000
